About us

Do you want the chance to gain work experience and develop your skills in an entrepreneurial way via networking with amazing global partners such as Amazon, HSBC, Visa, Asda and so many others, whilst helping people in the local community? If you answered yes, then Enactus Manchester is perfect for you! Enactus stands for 'Entrepreneurial Action Through Us.' It is a global student-led organisation which develops and implements social enterprise projects with the aim of giving students a chance to use their degree skills as well as their business acumen to help empower others.


Our Projects


Blossom

We empower refugee women in Britain through making and selling artistic products. Our workshops provide the materials and guidance to support their creational process. Blossom seeks to help these women develop transferable skills, nurture a window for catharsis, and smoother societal integration.

EmpowerMEal

We wish to help the marginalized women of Manchester. We deliver fresh food and food boxes to the students that wish to balance their diet and to live a healhty lifestyle at an affordable price. Our beneficiaries are in charge of preparing and assembling the boxes that will be delivered to you right on time.

Next Step

We've done our research on the gaps in the current self-improvement sector. We aim to create a beautiful interactive workbook designed to help you implement all the best practices and techniques proven to work by psychologists. The revenue generated will be used to help the homeless become financially independent.
